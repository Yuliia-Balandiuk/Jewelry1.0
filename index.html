About us

Shine Boutique can help make your special moment truly memorable. We offer unique pieces with the best diamonds available.

At Shine Boutique we've found a perfect balance between classic diamonds and modern styles. Our craftsmen present the latest trends in modern design. We can advise you on selecting the best shape, cut, style of the ring for your beloved. If you have a particular ring in mind or want to work with our craftsmen on a custom design and create something personal, we offer only the best quality.

Our services include finding the perfect stone, design, gem-cutting, remodeling, and restoration of your old jewelry pieces. We work only with high-quality gems that allows us to implement even the most incredible ideas. All of our rings have a lifetime warranty, so if anything happens to the ring, just bring it to our workshop.
